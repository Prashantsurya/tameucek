ABOUT CONFERENCE



This conference will provide a platform for academicians, researchers, scientists and industry personnel from industry to come together and discuss the latest developments in the broad areas of mechanical engineering .This will help the researchers to publish their research papers and provides a platform for future research activities in the above fields. The main focus of TAME - 2016 is to integrate innovations in mechanical engineering leading to rapid discovery, development, and application of new products for practical use.

ABOUT JNTU KAKINADA



The Engineering College, Kakinada was established in the year 1946 in composite Madras state as a Government Engineering College and subsequently became one of the constituent colleges of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University in the year 1972. From 2008, University College of Engineering has been the lead constituent college of the newly formed JNT University Kakinada.

ABOUT MECHANICAL ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT


The Department of mechanical engineering prioritizes the necessary revamping of mechanical engineering education which is driven primarily by dynamics of technological advances and sustainable development and with active involvement of industry, alumni, research organizations and many other stake holders. Academic programmes leading to B.Tech degree in Mechanical Engineering and M.Tech in Machine Design, CAD/CAM, Thermal Engg. and full time/part time Ph.D are being offered by the department.
